About Me

Hello, and welcome to my portfolio. This is my first crack at web development, so I hope I'm off on the right foot! A little about myself — my name is Brian, I'm 31 years old, grew up in a small town in central Pennsylvania, and have been living in Philadelphia for the better part of the last 12 years. I have two older brothers, a father originally from New York, and a mother originally from Vietnam. I don't have any pets right now, but I want a little Shih Tzu someday!

I'm excited to start this journey into the world of coding and web development. I've always had a strong interest in computer science, but at the same time, was a bit intimidated by it all. I went to Temple University, got a liberal arts degree, got a job, and have been toiling away ever since. I've been very fortunate for all of those things, but have been yearning for something more. So I finally put those fears to rest, and I'm ready to take on a new challenge in my life! That excitement definitely comes with its share of anxiety, but I'm quickly learning that I have a deeper interest and eagerness than I had anticipated. I hope that's a good sign!
